Get to Know Us

Meet the Baker

Hi, I'm Amanda! As a mother of three and a lifelong lover of baking, I started The Sweeter Life to share my passion for creating beautiful, delicious desserts with my community.

Every treat I make is crafted from scratch with quality ingredients and a whole lot of love. From custom birthday cakes to elegant wedding desserts, I believe every celebration deserves something sweet and special.

Based in the San Gabriel Valley, I'm proud to serve families and events throughout the Greater Los Angeles area. Let me help make your next occasion a little sweeter!
